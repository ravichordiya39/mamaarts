Top Garment export manufacturing house

Since 1994, Saivana Exports Private Limited is a premium export manufacturing house based in New Delhi, India. The company specializes in the production of very high fashion ladies and children garments and is today dealing with some of the top designers and fashion houses across the globe- especially from France, Italy, and the United States of America.

Top Indian garment exporter –  Most of the garments it manufactures are complicated and difficult, each having multiple value-added operations on them.

Saivana has Three divisions

  1. American Division – Handling clients Ulla Johnson / Tory Burch
  2. European Division – Handling clients Isabel Marant / Bonpoint
  3. Emerging Division – Handles small / startup brands across the globe. This is one of the most successful divisions of the company where Saivana invests all its resources in helping build and grow the brand. Currently the division handles 17 brands from Australia, Norway, France and the United States of America. Saivana also engages independent third party quality control inspector and all its shipment at its own expenses.

The Company invests heavily each year on advanced TQM (Total Quality Management) systems. From the fabric stage to packing, each garment is checked 100% for defects. In 16 years of production, Saivana is proud to say that it has never experienced any major quality issues with any of its buyers. All deliveries are having a 99% error free production.

The strengths of the Company are both in basic garments and in value additions like embroideries (machine, computer and hand), block prints and hand painted fabrics.

Depending on season to season and on the buyer, the Company works with all kinds of fabrics like fine cotton voiles, cambric’s, silk chiffons, georgettes, etc. The Company does constant research on new fabrics and embroidery techniques which are then incorporated by the buyers in their own styles and collections

Saivana has taken care to concentrate on the middle up markets segments of the global fashion industry. Today, majority of its buyers like Isabel Marant and Bonpoint have been associated with Saivana for more than a decade due to its strict commitment to delivering an excellent quality product within the required time frame of delivery
